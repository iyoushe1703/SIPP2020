SIPP: AN INTRODUCTION

Startup Internship Placement Programme or SIPP is a part of the flagship entrepreneurship celebration gala of EDC, BVUCOE called E-Summit.
A lot of engineering graduates struggle to find jobs matching their profiles. Similar is the case for finding internships. Leveraging its far-reaching corporate relations to bridge this gap between job finders and job providers, EDC started SIPP. Companies looking for employees and interns are hence invited to conduct their interviewing processes and recruit candidates of their choice.

Skills that companies generally look for...

WEB DEVELOPMENT

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS etc.

APP DEVELOPMENT

iOS and Android

CONTENT WRITING

English Proficiency, Business Communication.


SALES AND MARKETING

Social Media Marketing, Digital Marketing, E-mail Marketing.

English Proficiency, MS-Office, Tally etc.

GRAPHIC DESIGNING

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Fireworks etc.
